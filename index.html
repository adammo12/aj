Current position

Professor

Head of the Data Science group at the Department of Computer Science, University of Innsbruck.

Research Interests

  • Knowledge extraction from temporal document collections
  • Comprehension-focused search
  • Retrieval-augmented Large Language Models
  • Large Language Models
